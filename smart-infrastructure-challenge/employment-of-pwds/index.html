    Employment of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in the Philippines – Study

    As part of our submissions for the 2018 Smart Infrastructure Challenge, our team has chosen to include mobility access for PWDs into the scope of our portfolio of projects. In addition to the disability access of newly emerging vehicles within the PUV modernization program, we are also proposing a low-cost wheelchair lift installed on pedestrian overpasses near the hospitals within the region.

    Thank you to Richard Arceno of the World Disability Union for making this study available to us.
